Hey there! My name's Dan and I'm a software engineer and aspiring product designer from San Francisco.

Below you'll find a collection of some of my recent projects, both professional and otherwise. Most are things I picked up in my spare time. Granted I'm not a master welder, carpenter, game designer, or electrical engineer, but I am a determined and enthusiastic pretender.

1806 Cocktails

My most recent and largest scale project. Got to wear lots of hats: lead designer, iOS developer, and backend engineer.

Bolt: Mobile Ad Creator Tool

Bolt was my main focus during my time at Conversant. The web app helped internal ad designers quickly build interactive video, flash, and custom HTML5 ad formats, which ran on both iOS and Android.

MIDI Drumpad

A foray into Arduino tinkering and hardware design. Please excuse my lack of rhythm.

Set! for iOS

For some reason, Set Enterprises didn't always offer an iPhone version of their game. So I went ahead and made one.

Emily Capo Photography
